# FishyGame

Dive into the depths of an underwater world in **FishyGame**, a thrilling Java-based adventure where you control a brave fish navigating treacherous aquariums filled with enemies, bosses, and hidden treasures. Battle fierce predators like sharks and barracudas, collect power-ups such as heart items and stun bubbles, and unleash attacks with bombs and bubbles to survive and thrive.

Key Features:
- **Dynamic Gameplay**: Explore multiple levels with unique environments and challenges.
- **Variety of Fish**: Choose from different fish types, each with their own abilities and animations.
- **Combat System**: Engage in epic battles using attack bubbles, rain bubbles, and special weapons.
- **Sound and Visuals**: Immerse yourself with custom sounds, particle effects, and vibrant graphics.
- **Collision Detection**: Precise mechanics ensure fair and exciting encounters.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore adventurer, FishyGame offers endless fun in the aquatic realm. Get ready to swim, fight, and conquer the ocean!

## Levels

FishyGame features four challenging levels, each with increasing difficulty, unique enemies, and escalating win scores. Progress through the underwater world by defeating monsters and reaching the required score!

- **Level 1**: The beginner's dive! Face off against smaller fish like minnows, surgeonfish, and lionfish. Aim for a win score of 2,000 points to advance. Perfect for learning the ropes and building your fish's strength.
  
- **Level 2**: Deeper waters bring tougher challenges. Encounter barracuda, parrotfish, and sharks. Boost your score to 5,000 points to unlock the next stage. Watch out—these predators are faster and more aggressive!

- **Level 3**: The ocean's elite awaits. Battle Tuna, Pufferfish, and John Dory in this intense level. Reach 10,000 points to prove your dominance. Special abilities and higher health make this a true test of skill.

- **Level 4**: The ultimate showdown! No regular monsters here—just an epic boss battle with a win score of 99,999 points. Use all your collected power-ups and strategies to emerge victorious and complete the game.

Each level introduces new environmental hazards and rewards, so adapt your tactics as you go!

## Getting Started
- **Requirements**: Java 8 or higher, compatible with Windows/Linux/Mac.
- **How to Run**: Compile and run the `FishyGame.java` file in the `src/main/` directory.
- **Controls**: Use keyboard and mouse for movement, attacks, and interactions (see in-game UI for details).

For more details, check out the source code in the `src/` folder or contribute to the project!
